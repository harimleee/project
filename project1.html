PAINTING FOR THE FUTURE

It is a website that sponsors children in need through purchase and subscription of paintings.
By selling paintings by children themselves,
Children don't have the perception that they're being sponsored out of pity.
He recognized that he was being sponsored by his paintings.
I planned this project with the intention to enhance the self-esteem of nature.
This site was a team project and was inspired by Toms' shoe donation system through the
purchase of sneakers.
Design, html, and css work were done as a team, and functions through JavaScript were
carried out as a personal work.

SUGGECTIONS

01. UI/UX

The use of pastel tones rich in color gives the donation site a warm feeling.
Since there are many photo images, the background color should be a calm color that is not noticeable.
Where an illustration needs to be felt, it gives the impression of drawing using an illustration.
To give consistency to the entire site, the main menu, footer, and anchored icon do not change as the page changes.
The size of the image is defined according to the grid of the site.
It is visually expressable that it is a site that sponsors children by putting children's photos.

02. SCREEN COMPOSITION

Overall, it is composed of a sense of unity.
The menu simplifies and enables you to induce subscriptions and purchases.

03. REACTION TYPE OF 768px OR LESS

Header hides with menu icon.
The pictures in the shop window are arranged in pairs.
The questions section of the faq window falls down to one by one by one.
The icons of join us all fall down to one.
Everything that used to be the left and right alignment of the shop's detail window is arranged downwards.
